Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Vocab Builder ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Vocab Builder.

2. Data Collection

We prioritize your data privacy. Most data created within the app (word lists, definitions, progress) is stored locally on your device.

  • Personal Data: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Backup Data: If you use the backup feature, your data is stored in the location you select (local storage).

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage/Files: Required to backup and restore your vocabulary lists (`.zip` files).
  • Billing: Required to process one-time premium purchases via Google Play Billing.
  • Internet: Required to fetch definitions and verify purchase signatures.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and billing.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Instructions & FAQ

How do I backup my data?

Go to the "More" screen, select "Backup & Restore," and choose "Export Data". This will create a secure ZIP file on your device.

Is the dictionary offline?

Yes. Once the initial dictionary database is downloaded, you can search for words without an internet connection.
