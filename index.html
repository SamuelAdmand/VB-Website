Tutorials

Adding a Custom Dictionary

Follow these steps to integrate external web dictionaries into the app:

  1. Navigate to the Settings screen in the app.
  2. Find the section for managing dictionaries and select "Add Custom Dictionary".
  3. You will be prompted to enter:
    • Dictionary Name: A short name (e.g., "Longman").
    • URL: The search URL containing the {query} placeholder.

Ready-to-Use Dictionary URLs

Copy and paste these directly into the app:

Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary

https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/{query}

Longman Dictionary of Contemporary English

https://www.ldoceonline.com/dictionary/{query}

Instructions & FAQ

How do I backup my data?

Go to the "More" screen, select "Backup & Restore," and choose "Export Data". This will create a secure ZIP file on your device.

Is the dictionary offline?

Yes. Once the initial dictionary database is downloaded, you can search for words without an internet connection.
