App Tutorials

Learn how to get the most out of Vocab Builder.

Adding a Custom Dictionary

Vocab Builder allows you to integrate external web dictionaries directly into the app's lookup flow. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and navigate to the Settings screen.
  2. Scroll to the "Dictionaries" section and tap "Add Custom Dictionary".
  3. You will be prompted to enter:
    • Dictionary Name: A short label (e.g., "Longman").
    • Dictionary URL: The search URL containing the special {query} placeholder.
  4. Tap Save. You can now select this dictionary from the search results screen.

Ready-to-Use URLs

You can copy and paste these URLs directly into the app:

Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary

https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/{query}

Longman Dictionary of Contemporary English

https://www.ldoceonline.com/dictionary/{query}

Backup & Restore

To keep your data safe, we recommend creating regular backups.

  1. Go to the More tab.
  2. Select Backup & Restore.
  3. Tap Export Data to save a secure .zip file to your device.
